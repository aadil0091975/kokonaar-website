About KOKONAAR

We are dedicated to providing sustainable, high-quality agricultural and industrial products derived entirely from coconuts. By harnessing the natural strength and water retention capabilities of coconut byproducts, we offer eco-friendly solutions for gardening, agriculture, and soil erosion control.

Why Choose Our Cocopeat?

The Substrate

100% natural, organic, and biodegradable. Our cocopeat holds up to 10x its weight in water, providing excellent aeration and drainage for superior root growth.

Available Formats

We supply cocopeat in various formats to suit your needs: 5kg blocks, 650g briquettes, grow bags, and loose in bulk bags.

Production

Meticulously processed through washing, sieving, and drying to ensure low EC and neutral pH levels before compression.
